# Lead Scoring & Automated Nurturing

Turning raw leads into qualified opportunities.

## 1. Lead Scoring Framework
Assign points based on data and behavior:
- **Demographics (+10 pts)**: Matches target profile.
- **Intent (+20 pts)**: Requested a specific quote or demo.
- **Engagement (+5 pts)**: Opened an email or clicked a link.
- **Urgency (+15 pts)**: Expressed interest in immediate start/purchase.
*Threshold*: If Lead Score > 50, mark as "Hot Lead" and notify a senior closer.

## 2. The Welcome Nurture Sequence (Email)
Triggered upon form submission:
- **Email 1 (Instant)**: The "Thank You" + Value Delivery (Brochure/Link).
- **Email 2 (Day 1)**: "Why Us?" - Focus on trust, authority, and USP.
- **Email 3 (Day 3)**: Social Proof - Testimonials or Case Studies.
- **Email 4 (Day 5)**: FAQ & Objection Handling - Addressing common concerns.
- **Email 5 (Day 7)**: The "Direct Offer" - Invitation to a discovery call or trial.

## 3. Re-engagement Workflows
For leads that haven't responded in 14 days:
- **"Just Checking In"**: Ask if they have any remaining questions.
- **New Updates/Promo**: Notify them of new product features or limited-time offers.
- **The "Good-bye" Email**: "Is this still a priority?" - Often triggers a last-minute conversion.

## 4. Automation for Sales Efficiency
- **Internal Notifications**: Send Slack/Email alerts only for high-priority leads.
- **Task Creation**: Automatically create a "Follow-up" task in the CRM for the assigned rep.
- **Data Enrichment**: Use external APIs to automatically find the lead's LinkedIn profile or company info (if B2B).

## Optimization Metrics
- **Time to First Contact**: Goal: < 10 minutes.
- **Lead-to-Qualified Rate**: % of raw leads that meet the scoring threshold.
- **Sequence Open/Click Rates**: Identifying which nurture emails are underperforming.
