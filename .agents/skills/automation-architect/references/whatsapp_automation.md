# WhatsApp & Instant Communication Guide

Implementing high-conversion automated communication via WhatsApp.

## 1. WhatsApp Business API (Official)
- **Message Templates**: Must be pre-approved by Meta. Use for the initial "Welcome" message.
- **Dynamic Variables**: `Hello {{1}}, thank you for your interest in {{2}}...`
- **Interactive Buttons**: Use "Call-to-Action" (e.g., "Chat with Admin") or "Quick Reply" (e.g., "Send Price List") buttons.

## 2. Instant Follow-up Logic
- **Trigger**: Form Submission.
- **Action**: Immediate (0-2 minutes) WhatsApp notification to the lead.
- **Notification**: Instant alert to the sales rep via WhatsApp/Slack with the lead's details.

## 3. The "Hybrid" Approach
- **Phase 1 (Auto)**: Instant template message with a link to a PDF or a "Thank You" video.
- **Phase 2 (Human)**: Auto-assign the lead to a rep; the rep sends a personal follow-up within 30 minutes.

## 4. Scheduling Automation
- **Calendar Integration**: Use tools like Calendly or SavvyCal to allow leads to book a consultation directly from the WhatsApp message.
- **Reminders**: Send automated reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before the scheduled call.

## Communication Best Practices
- **Tone**: Professional, helpful, and culturally appropriate.
- **Timing**: Respect "Quiet Hours" (e.g., 9 PM - 7 AM) unless the user specifically opted into immediate contact.
- **Unsubscribe**: Provide a clear "Opt-out" path to maintain compliance and brand trust.
